## Historic Sites

### [Beaumaris Castle](https://cadw.gov.wales/visit/places-to-visit/beaumaris-castle)
Edward I's unfinished masterpiece ‚Äî a UNESCO World Heritage Site and perhaps the greatest castle never completed.
- Near-perfect symmetrical design with concentric defences
- Water-filled moat with its own dock
- 300 arrow loops in the outer walls alone
- Free public WiFi on site
- Excellent crabbing nearby:
  - [Best at high tide](https://www.thebeachguide.co.uk/north-wales/anglesey/beaumaris-weather.htm)
  - Buy equipment (~¬£12) near the pier or sweet shop
  - Bait: cheap bacon from Spar works great
  - Fill bucket with seawater, add stones as weights
- Kids playground in town
- [Puffin Island cruises](#puffin-island--wildlife-cruises) depart from the pier

### [Bryn Celli Ddu](https://cadw.gov.wales/visit/places-to-visit/bryn-celli-ddu-chambered-tomb)
One of Britain's most evocative prehistoric monuments ‚Äî a 5,000-year-old burial chamber.
- Walk inside the ancient passage tomb
- Originally a henge with a stone circle
- Famous for its summer solstice alignment ‚Äî sunlight illuminates the inner chamber
- Free entry, managed by Cadw
- Guided tours available May-August
- Near Llanddaniel Fab

### [Plas Newydd](https://www.nationaltrust.org.uk/visit/wales/plas-newydd-house-and-garden)
Stunning National Trust house and gardens on the banks of the Menai Strait.
- Grade 1 listed ancestral home of the Marquess of Anglesey
- Rex Whistler's famous 58ft mural
- 40 acres of gardens with Snowdonia views
- Over 100 red squirrels in the grounds!
- Disc golf course and playground
- Caf√©, shop and ice cream
- EV charging points available
- Dogs welcome in most garden areas

## Unique Experiences

### [Parys Mountain](https://maps.app.goo.gl/eSW1vpRxTuRA3344A)
A surreal, Mars-like landscape ‚Äî the remains of what was once the world's largest copper mine.
- Otherworldly coloured rocks and pools
- Easy walking trails around the old workings
- Free to explore
- [See the incredible views](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Mynydd+Parys)

### [South Stack Lighthouse](https://www.trinityhouse.co.uk/lighthouse-visitor-centres/south-stack-lighthouse-visitor-centre)
Dramatic clifftop lighthouse on Holy Island with spectacular seabird colonies.
- 400 steps down to the lighthouse (and back up!)
- [RSPB Nature Reserve](https://www.rspb.org.uk/days-out/reserves/south-stack) ‚Äî 10,000+ breeding seabirds in summer
- See puffins, guillemots, razorbills and choughs
- Ellin's Tower viewing centre with telescopes and live CCTV
- Dolphins spotted occasionally
- Caf√© serving coffee and lunch
- Tiny play area tucked around the back with sea views

### [Melin Llynnon](https://www.visitanglesey.co.uk/en-gb/explore/llynon-mill)
The only working windmill in Wales, dating from 1775.
- Stone tower mill near Llanddeusant
- Reconstructed Iron Age roundhouses on site
- 2-mile family-friendly Llynon Trail
- **Monuts** ‚Äî incredible artisan doughnuts!
- Note: Mill sails not currently operating

### Stone Science
Hands-on geology and fossil experience.
- [Location on Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/72MJssQhs98WMCpb6)
